Projects / Activities

A Modern Solution to Silent Struggles: Developing a Guidance Incident Reporting System for Gatid Elementary School

The purpose of this project comes from actual feedback and observations gathered from the school environment. Teachers and guidance counselors at Gatid Elementary School have conveyed their concerns over the restricted effectiveness of the present-day current incident reporting procedure. In several cases, issues such as bullying, behavioral issues, and peer conflicts remain unaddressed in terms of students being unwilling to approach adults directly.
